
Filter the term meta shown in the metabox:

'jsmstm_metabox_table_metadata' ( $metadata, $term_obj )

An array of regular expressions to exclude meta keys:

'jsmstm_metabox_table_skip_keys' ( $skip_keys, $term_obj )

current_user_can() capability required to show term meta:

'jsmstm_show_metabox_capability' ( 'manage_options', $term_obj )

Show term meta for this taxonomy (defaults to true):

'jsmstm_show_metabox_taxonomy' ( true, $taxonomy )

current_user_can() capability required to delete term meta:

'jsmstm_delete_meta_capability' ( 'manage_options', $term_obj )
